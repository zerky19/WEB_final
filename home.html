Hayao Miyazaki

The director approaches the creation of the worlds of his cartoons with special meticulousness. Wherever events unfold - in Japan, in idealized seaside Europe, or in post-apocalyptic forests and deserts - each time the viewer is transported to a new world, both unfamiliar and recognizable thanks to a multitude of everyday details.
